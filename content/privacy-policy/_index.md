# Privacy Policy - Meetball Handbook

**TL;DR:** This handbook is a static documentation site. We collect zero data about you.

## What This Policy Covers

This privacy policy is specific to **handbook.meetball.fun** (this site you're reading right now).

For Meetball's general privacy practices across all our services and applications, see our [Company Privacy Policy](https://earlyfans.meetball.fun/privacy-policy).

---

## Data Collection on This Handbook

Here's what we do (and don't do):

### What We DON'T Collect
- ❌ **No cookies** - We don't set any cookies
- ❌ **No analytics** - No Google Analytics, no tracking pixels, nothing
- ❌ **No personal information** - No emails, names, or any identifying data
- ❌ **No tracking** - We don't follow you around the web
- ❌ **No advertising** - No ad networks, no remarketing
- ❌ **No form data** - This is a static site with no forms

### How This Site Works
- ✅ **Fully static** - Generated with Zola, just plain HTML/CSS/JS
- ✅ **Client-side search** - Search runs entirely in your browser (elasticlunr.js)
- ✅ **No server-side processing** - We serve files, that's it
- ✅ **Open source** - You can see exactly how it works on [GitHub](https://github.com/himeetball/handbook)

---

## Hosting & Infrastructure

### Cloudflare Pages
This handbook is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may collect technical information as part of providing their service, including:
- IP addresses (for DDoS protection and service delivery)
- Request headers
- Performance metrics

This data is governed by [Cloudflare's Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/), not ours.

### Third-Party Libraries
We use several open-source libraries for functionality (search, math rendering, etc.). These run in your browser and don't send data anywhere. See our [third-party licenses](/third-party-licenses.txt) for details.

---

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there's nothing for us to:
- Access
- Modify
- Delete
- Export

If you're concerned about Cloudflare's data collection, you can:
- Use a VPN
- Use privacy-focused browsers
- Contact Cloudflare directly about their practices

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our data collection practices (like adding analytics), we'll:
1. Update this page with clear information
2. Update the effective date below
3. Probably announce it somewhere (we believe in transparency)

We won't retroactively collect data. Any changes will be prospective only.
