# Vision

### **A world where the most meaningful relationships begin with “How can I help?”**

Our **Vision** is to create a world where people make meaningful connections through shared purpose, tapping into our natural desire to connect and help each other.

> ***We were born to read eyes, not replies.***
>
> *To trade whispers, not algorithms.*
>
> *To hold hands, not handles.*
>
> *We were fluent in silence, before the noise of notifications drowned our names.*
>
> *And before the world forgets,*
>
> *And the blue light flashes all dreams away,*
>
> *Let's simply be us one more time.*

# Mission

### Help people make meaningful connections through shared interests.

Our mission is to create a space where asking for help is celebrated, offering it is second nature, and real connections happen.

We turn events and communities into places where strangers become collaborators, mentors, and friends by connecting people through shared interests, creating a safe space where people can share what’s on their mind and find the people who can help.

We realise the Mission by building our App, and beyond that, by sharing our open Recipe Book to inspire other companies who want to work this way.

### **We’ll start by living the Mission ourselves:**

We're building this startup by embodying the very connections *WE* want to create. Our organization is our first product, a living demonstration of our mission in action.

We operate with an open contribution model where we ask "Can you help?" and anyone can respond "I can help!".

By sharing our mission, articulating our needs and asking those who resonate to help us, we’re building this startup using the very framework for fostering collaboration that our platform will offer our users.

This alignment between how we work and our product creates unique advantages: The builders of the solution innately understand the problem we're solving, because we live it daily, and we create a community where users naturally become contributors who become team members.

How we work and our app user experience help people realise their dreams by contributing to something that serves their mission.
