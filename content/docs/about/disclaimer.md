## The Real Talk

Look, we need to be clear about something: **this handbook is us sharing our journey**, not us telling you how to run your company.

### What This Handbook Is

This is our recipe book. It's how **we** at Meetball work, based on our lived experience building an anti-social network. We're sharing it openly because transparency is cool and maybe it'll help someone.

But here's the thing: **your kitchen is different than ours**.

### What This Handbook Isn't

This handbook is **NOT**:
- ❌ Professional business advice
- ❌ Legal counsel (seriously, talk to a lawyer if you need one)
- ❌ Financial guidance
- ❌ HR consulting
- ❌ A guaranteed formula for success
- ❌ Recommendations suitable for your specific situation

We're just a team figuring things out as we go. Sometimes we get it right, sometimes we learn the hard way. That's life.

## Before You Copy-Paste Our Ideas

### Talk to Actual Professionals

If something in here inspires you and you want to try it at your organization, **please** consult with:
- A lawyer for legal stuff
- An HR professional for workplace things
- A financial advisor for money matters
- Licensed consultants for business strategy

We're sharing what works for us in our specific context. Your context is probably different.

### Do Your Homework

Before implementing anything you read here:
1. Check what laws apply in your location
2. Think about your specific situation
3. Consider the risks and benefits for **your** team
4. Maybe test it on a small scale first?
5. Adapt it to fit your needs

Don't just copy-paste. That rarely works.
