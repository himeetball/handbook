## What This Handbook Is

This handbook documents how **we** at Meetball work. It reflects our lived experience, our experiments, and our evolving approach to building an anti-social network together.

We're sharing this openly because:
- **Transparency helps everyone learn** - If our experiments help others, awesome
- **We believe in building in public** - Inspired by Basecamp, GitLab, and other open organizations
- **Accountability** - Writing down how we work helps us work better

## What This Handbook Isn't

**This is not prescriptive advice.** What works for us might not work for you.

Every team is different:
- Different people, different contexts
- Different stages, different challenges
- Different cultures, different laws

We share this because it helps us, and maybe it'll inspire you. But your mileage will probably vary.

## Living Document

This handbook is continuously evolving. We update it as we:
- Learn from our mistakes
- Experiment with new approaches
- Grow and adapt to new challenges

If something seems outdated or unclear, it probably needs updating. Feel free to suggest changes!

## Using This Handbook

### For Meetball Team Members
This is your guide. Read it, question it, improve it. If something doesn't make sense or doesn't match reality, that's a bug - help us fix it.

### For Others
You're welcome to:
- Learn from our approach
- Adapt ideas for your own team
- Share it with others (with attribution)
- Suggest improvements

we suggest: **consult appropriate professionals** (legal, HR, financial) before implementing anything in your organization. We're not consultants, just sharing our journey.
