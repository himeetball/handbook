## How to Get Your @meetball.fun Email

Once you've signed our Confidentiality Agreement (NDA), you can request your very own @meetball.fun email address.

### Step 1: Request Your Email

**Go to:** [ineedemail.meetball.xyz](https://ineedemail.meetball.xyz)

Fill out the form with:
- **Your chosen email name**: We suggest `firstname@meetball.fun` (we're a small crew!), but `firstnamelastname@meetball.fun` works too
- **Recovery email**: Must match the email you used to sign the NDA on BoldSign (this verifies it's you)
- **Phone recovery**: Optional, but not recommended (SMS isn't secure)

### Step 2: Submit

Click **Request Email** and wait for approval.

### Step 3: Get Approved

Guilherme will:
- Verify you're eligible (NDA signed, details match)
- Approve and create the email
- DM you on Discord with:
  - Your new @meetball.fun email address
  - Login credentials

## Setting Up Your Email

**Important:** This email provider is for team emails only.

### First Login

**Recommended:** Reset your password on first login for security.

👉 [Change your password here](https://purelymail.com/user/changePassword)

### Using Email on Your Devices

Your @meetball.fun email works with any email client that supports IMAP and SMTP.

#### Recommended Email Clients:
- **Mobile**: Gmail, Apple Mail, BlueMail, K-9 Mail
- **Desktop**: Thunderbird, Apple Mail, Outlook

#### Setup Instructions

**1. Add Account**
- Open your email client
- Go to Settings → Add Account
- Select **Other** or **Manual Setup**
- Choose **IMAP**

**2. Incoming Mail Server (IMAP)**
- **Email**: your@meetball.fun
- **Password**: [your password]
- **Server**: `imap.purelymail.com`
- **Port**: 993 (usually auto-detected)
- **Security**: SSL/TLS (usually auto-detected)

**3. Outgoing Mail Server (SMTP)**
- **Email**: your@meetball.fun
- **Password**: [your password]
- **Server**: `smtp.purelymail.com`
- **Port**: 465 (usually auto-detected)
- **Security**: SSL/TLS (usually auto-detected)

**4. Sync Settings**
- Leave defaults or choose your preferred sync frequency
- Set sync time to as early/frequent as your client allows

**That's it!** Your @meetball.fun email is ready to use.
