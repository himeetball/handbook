**Before you ship, publish, or share… ask:** 

---

## 1. Is it Human?  
- Does this feel like something a real person would say or do?  
- Are we speaking clearly, not performing?  

---

## 2. Is it Generous?  
- Does this invite people in?  
- Are we leading with curiosity or offering help, not asking for attention?  

---

## 3. Is it Connection-First?  
- Are we helping people find each other — or just broadcasting?  
- Does it reduce friction and make real-world connection easier?  

---

## 4. Is it Community-Driven?  
- Does this reflect how people use and shape Meetball?  
- Are we amplifying real voices, not just pushing a message?  

---

## 5. Is it Aligned with Our Values?  
- Does it reflect our core values? (i.e. openness, integrity, mutual support, and building what’s missing)  
- Are we prioritizing meaningful impact over hype/vanity?
